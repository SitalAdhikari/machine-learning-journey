## Day 86: Moving to Google Colab & Connecting Google Drive
Today, I transitioned my workflow from local Jupyter Notebooks to **Google Colab** and mastered cloud-based data fetching.
### Key Learnings
 * **Environment Shift**: Switched from traditional local Jupyter Notebooks to cloud-based Google Colab for training models.
 * **Google Drive Integration**: Learned to mount personal Google Drive storage directly inside a Colab environment instance.
 * **Data Access Pipeline**: Programmed paths to seamlessly fetch and feed hosted datasets straight into code notebooks.
 * **Key Advantages**: Outlined major cloud execution benefits, including zero local environment installation requirements, free access to powerful GPU/TPU hardware accelerators, and seamless project sharing capabilities.
